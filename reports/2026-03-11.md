- [Developing Optimal CUDA Kernels on Hopper (PDF)](https://www.kapilsharma.dev/assets/data/nvidia_hopper_presentation.pdf) — 2026-02-18
  - Authors: n/a
  - Affiliations: not provided
  - CUTLASS 3 frames Hopper GEMM kernels as two composable pieces: a **mainloop** (tile-wise MMA + async data movement) and an **epilogue** (post-processing + writeback), making GEMM a natural unit for **kernel fusion**.
  - The **epilogue is the primary fusion surface**: you can fuse bias, activation, scaling, type conversion, and reductions directly into the GEMM writeback to cut extra kernels and global-memory traffic.
  - Hopper-oriented kernel design emphasizes **pipelined mainloops** (e.g., async copies / multi-stage buffering) to keep Tensor Cores fed, while epilogue fusion helps maintain end-to-end throughput by avoiding bandwidth-bound post-GEMM passes.
