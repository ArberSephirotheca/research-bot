## arXiv: AI/ML + GPU (new: 1, showing: 1)
- [TiledAttention: a CUDA Tile SDPA Kernel for PyTorch](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.01960v1) — 2026-03-02
  - Authors: Taimur Khan
  - Affiliations: not provided
  - Implements SDPA forward as a tiled CUDA kernel with streamed \(K,V\) and online softmax, matching realistic fused-attention behavior while avoiding full materialization of the attention matrix (reduces bandwidth/DRAM traffic vs eager paths).
  - Built in cuTile/TileIR and callable from PyTorch; practitioners can edit schedule-level tiling/staging/shared-memory layouts from Python (no CUTLASS-style template rewrites), enabling rapid kernel research around GEMM-like QK\(^T\) and AV matmul phases.
  - Benchmarks on DGX GB10 show strong speedups over unfused/eager attention across seq length, head dim, and FP16/BF16, though still generally behind best production fused SDPA kernels.
